Antibiotics vs Probiotics

Based on the opinion of Lyndon Lewis and not fact.

Antibiotics

The word "anti" means against and the word "biotic" refers to Life, the condition of living organisims. So combined, the word "antibiotic" means "against life." In the medical field, "antibiotics" is usually the medicine/drug that is prescribed to kill and or cure a specific harmful infection or bacteria. The gut is full of bacteria (both good and bad) that are used by the body in the digestive process or as a part of the digestive system. Antibiotics will kill them both so the digestive process/system could potentially suffer as a result of it's use or over use.

Probiotics

Probiotics are live microorganisms that are intended to have health benefits. Although people often think of bacteria and other microorganisms as harmful "germs", many help our bodies to function properly. For example, there are microorganisms/bacteria in our intestines that help to digest food, destroy disease-causing microorganisms, and produce vitamins.
